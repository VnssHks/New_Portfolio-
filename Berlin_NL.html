Berlin Neighbourhood Map

This was created as part of an independent project. According to my sister's wishes for the design and the locations to be included on the map, I create a simple Berlin street map and overlayed it with illustrations of her favorite spots around town.

The colors were chosen according to her new apartments color scheme, so the map would not stand out but rather fit perfectly into the other decoration.
