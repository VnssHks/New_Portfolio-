Neighbourhood Map

This was created as a gift for my friend's first apartment away from her parents. To commemorate the occasion, I took a look at all the important things around the neighbourhood and included them in a little bit of an artsy map.

The green and red colours were chosen, because they had just painted some parts of their apartment in a similar green, so to make sure the map could be displayed as an integrated piece of decoration, those colours were chosen.
