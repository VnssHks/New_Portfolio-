Mapping Origins of Migration

As part of a class at TU Vienna, we looked at the UN Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 10 - "reduce inequality within and among countries" and specifically target 7 - "facilitate orderly, safe, regular and responsible migration and mobility of people, including through the implementation of planned and well-managed migration policies".

In a group of four people, we looked at causes for migration and how it is connected to inequalities in both the country of origin and destination. To better approach the subject, and be able to explain the matter we decided to look at several individual stories of people who fled their home countries in hopes of a better future. The StoryMap aims to educate about the ongoing issue of inequalities around the world and proposes some solutions, all based on research and data. It includes several data visualizations and thematic maps surrounding the subject.
