"Ich hab' es Feuer regnen sehen." - "I've seen it rainin' fire"

This is an article that was published in the Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung as part of my Google News Fellowship there. The article is about the Perseid Meteor shower. It explains why this meteor shower is recurring every year, and includes a 3D animation of the comet that causes the shower, to better explain the phenomenon to the readers.

The map at the bottom of the page was made by analyzing light pollution data of Germany and then figuring out which areas the light pollution would be low or low enough to be able to see the meteors. Unfortunately in 2022, the peak of the shower happened to be at the same time as a full moon, so visibility was limited in general.
