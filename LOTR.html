One Map To Track Them All

As part of the mapping project class at TU Munich, we created a two-fold ArcGIS StoryMap. One part of the map shows the reader the filming-locations in New Zealand. On an interactive map, users could plan a trip to these locations, using the information we provided, such as whether there is an airport close, and how much a possible entrance fee is.

The other part of the map shows the characters routes from the movie "The Fellowship of the Ring" and invited the reader to interactively follow along their journey. The background of the map is comprised of handdrawn illustrations that are iterated many times to create a sort of Tolkien-esque map of Middle Earth. Users can choose between the different characters and take a detailed look into the ways they have come throughout the story of the first movie.
