Children's World Map

This was created as a gift for my cousin's first day of school. To make learning about the countries of the world more fun, I included handdrawn illustrations of some of the animals native to the countries.

Additionally, the flags of all the countries displayed on the map and their respective names and capitals were added to the bottom of the page. Of course a 6-year old wont actively try to learn all the countries of the world, however the simple presence of a huge map in her bedroom gives her the opportunity to learn little bits and pieces without having to actively sit down and study it. For simplicity, some of the smaller island nations were not included, but could be added if a more accurate World Map is needed for other purposes.
